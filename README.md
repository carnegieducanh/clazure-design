## What you can do

- Apply beautiful, ready-to-use card templates
- Drag & drop fields into Front / Back
- Customize font, colors, spacing for each field
- Preview changes instantly before applying
- Automatically backup & restore your templates
- Works on both light & dark mode

---

## How to use

1. Open Anki → **Tools → Clazure Design**
2. Select your Note Type
3. Choose a theme
4. Drag your fields
5. Click **Apply**

Done — your cards are now beautifully styled.
